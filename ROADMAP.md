# 🚀 Long-Term Vision

RIN is more than a voice assistant.

The vision is to create a private, intelligent AI companion capable of understanding natural language, automating daily tasks, managing the computer, remembering user preferences, and eventually evolving into a complete AI-powered desktop operating environment.

Every version of RIN brings the project one step closer to that goal.
