# 🤝 Contributing to RIN

Thank you for your interest in contributing to RIN.

RIN is a personal AI desktop assistant currently under active development.

The project is not production-ready, and its internal architecture may change
as new features are researched, tested, and integrated.

---

## Current Contribution Status

Community contributions are currently limited while the core architecture is
being developed.

Suggestions, bug reports, documentation improvements, and technical feedback
are welcome.

Large feature contributions may be postponed until the project reaches a more
stable milestone.

---
